Materials

If you're new to tie-dyeing, you might be surprised at how many different materials are required to create your own unique designs. To get started, you'll need a few key materials, including dye, fabric, rubber bands, and a large container for mixing and soaking the fabric. Depending on the specific technique you're using, you may also need additional tools like squeeze bottles or an artificial sinew puller. You'll also need a large container for mixing the dye and soaking the fabric, as well as access to water for rinsing and washing the fabric. As you become more experienced with tie-dyeing, you may find yourself experimenting with different materials and techniques to achieve different effects.
