Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information for those looking to get into the colorful world of tie-dye. Topics will cover many of the materials needed and what they are used for and where to get them, as well as different tying and dying techniques.

Audience

My audience is anyone looking to learn more about tie dying and getting started in this fun and creative hobby.

Home page

Welcome to Bright Eye Dyes! Here you will find a bounty of information for learning all about tie dye. You can find information regarding many of the required materials and where to buy them. And we'll even dive deeper into the different tying and dying techniques. Required Materials and Where to Find Them

Here we will cover the required (and optional) materials one can use to make all different types of tid dye.

For now, here's the straight list of items. I will refine this:

  • Artificial Sinew
  • Sinew puller
  • Kite string
  • Rubber bands
  • Washable markers
  • Clips
  • Mesh Basket
  • Plastic tubs
  • Hemostats
  • Needle tipped bottles
  • Squeeze bottles
  • Gloves
  • Measuring spoons
  • Measuring cups
  • Rags
  • Ruler
  • Chalk
  • Fishing line
  • Blank shirts of natural materials
  • Fiber Reactive Procion Dyes
  • Textile Detergent
  • Dust mask
  • Spray Bottle
